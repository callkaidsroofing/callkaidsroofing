## Quote Calculation Formula

```
Total Quote = Labor + Materials + Overhead + Margin
```

### Component Breakdown

1. **Labor Cost**
   - Hours Required × Labor Rate
   - Factor in site complexity (+10-20% for difficult access)

2. **Materials Cost**
   - Wholesale Price × Quantity × 1.30 (30% markup)
   - Include delivery fees if applicable

3. **Overhead (15%)**
   - Insurance, vehicle costs, admin
   - Calculated on (Labor + Materials)

4. **Profit Margin (20-25%)**
   - Standard margin on total
   - Can reduce for competitive jobs (min 15%)

### Example Quote Calculation

**Job:** Re-roof 120 sqm Colorbond

```
Labor: 24 hours × $85/hr = $2,040
Materials: 120 sqm × $35/sqm × 1.30 = $5,460
Overhead: ($2,040 + $5,460) × 0.15 = $1,125
Subtotal: $8,625
Margin (20%): $1,725
TOTAL QUOTE: $10,350
```

## Pricing Adjustments

### Volume Discounts
- 5-10% off for strata/multi-property contracts
- Return customer discount: 5%

### Complexity Premiums
- **High Pitch (>35°):** +15-25%
- **Multi-Story (3+ levels):** +20-30%
- **Heritage/Listed Property:** +30-50% (special requirements)
- **Difficult Access:** +10-20%

### Suburb Adjustments
- Standard SE Melbourne: Base rate
- Outer suburbs (+25km): +$100 travel surcharge
- CBD/High-Rise: Quote on assessment

## Payment Terms

**Standard:**
- 30% deposit on acceptance
- 40% mid-job (materials delivered)
- 30% on completion

**Small Jobs (<$1,500):**
- Full payment on completion

**Large Projects (>$10,000):**
- Custom payment schedule (3-5 milestones)

## Warranty Pricing

**Included in Quote:**
- 12-month workmanship warranty (no cost)
- Manufacturer material warranties (pass-through)

**Extended Warranties (Optional Add-On):**
- 5-year workmanship: +8% of quote
- 10-year workmanship: +15% of quote

## Emergency Call-Out

**After-Hours/Weekend:**
- Call-out fee: $250 (first hour)
- Hourly rate: $120/hour
- Emergency repairs prioritized within 4 hours

## Price Validity

All quotes valid for 30 days from issue date. Material costs subject to change beyond this period.
