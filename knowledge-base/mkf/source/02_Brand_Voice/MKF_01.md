# MKF_01 - Brand & Voice Mandate

## Brand Identity

**Call Kaids Roofing** is positioned as a modern, technology-enabled roofing contractor serving South East Melbourne. We combine traditional craftsmanship with AI-powered efficiency.

## Brand Colors

**Primary Palette:**
- **Primary Blue:** #007ACC (Digital trust, professionalism)
- **Dark Blue:** #0B3B69 (Authority, expertise)
- **Charcoal:** #111827 (Sophistication, durability)

**Usage Rules:**
- ✅ Use primary colors for branding and UI
- ❌ **NEVER use orange** (except safety/warning icons)
- White backgrounds for clarity, dark mode support required

## Typography

**Headings:** Serif fonts (traditional craftsmanship)  
**Body Text:** Montserrat (modern, readable)  
**UI Elements:** System fonts for performance

## Voice & Tone

### Communication Style

**Professional but Approachable**
- Use Australian English (colour, labour, metre)
- Date format: DD MMM YYYY (e.g., "15 Nov 2025")
- Phone format: 0435 900 909 (no parentheses or spaces in ABN)

**Be:**
- ✅ Clear and direct
- ✅ Solution-oriented
- ✅ Warm and helpful
- ✅ Technically accurate

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Corporate jargon
- ❌ Over-promising
- ❌ Vague language
- ❌ Pressure tactics

### Customer-Facing Messaging

**Inspection Booking:**
> "Book your free roof inspection today. We'll assess your roof's condition and provide a detailed quote with photos and recommendations."

**Quote Follow-up:**
> "Thanks for considering Call Kaids Roofing. Any questions about your quote? We're here to help clarify pricing, timeline, or scope of work."

**Job Completion:**
> "Your roof is complete! We've cleaned up the site and completed our safety checklist. Please review the work and let us know if you have any questions."

### Internal Communication

**Inspector Notes:** Technical, specific, photo-documented  
**Quote Justifications:** Reference pricing models and material costs  
**Team Coordination:** Clear task assignments with deadlines

## Brand Positioning

**Differentiators:**
1. **AI-Powered Operations** - Faster quotes, better scheduling
2. **Transparent Pricing** - Detailed breakdowns, no hidden fees
3. **Photo Documentation** - Before/after evidence for every job
4. **Warranty Support** - Digital records for easy warranty claims
5. **SE Melbourne Expertise** - Local knowledge of suburbs and conditions

## Taglines & Slogans

**Primary:** "Professional Roofing, Powered by AI"  
**Secondary:** "Your Trusted Roofing Partner in SE Melbourne"  
**Call-to-Action:** "Get Your Free Roof Inspection"

## Social Proof

> "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ [Customer Quote]" — [Name], [Suburb]

1. Problem (customer's roofing issue)
