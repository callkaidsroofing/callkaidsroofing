# CKR Warranty Policy — Industrial Roof Coatings
Effective: 10 Nov 2025

## Coverage
- **15‑Year Coating Warranty** — Standard.
- **20‑Year Coating Warranty** — Premium (specified system + prep checklist).
- Workmanship warranty applies per quote terms (typically 10 years).

## Conditions
1. Substrate prep per SOP (wash, repairs, prime, mask).
2. Ambient: 10–30 °C, wind < 30 km/h, no rain within 24 h post‑coat.
3. Approved system: industrial roof coatings line only.
4. Film build meets spec; batch numbers recorded.
5. Photo log: before/process/after.

## Exclusions
Structural issues, unrelated leaks, third‑party damage, neglect, non‑compliant repaints.

## Maintenance & Claims
Annual visual check + gutter clean recommended.
Claims via email with job ID and photos. CKR inspects and remedies where covered.
